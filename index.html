What it is

Sakuli is a open Platform for a variaty on E2E Scenarios including testing, monitoring or any other kind of automation. You can simulate the interaction of a real user with your system.

Sakuli 2 it is rewritten from groud-up with following points in mind:

  • Ready for the future
  • Open and extentible
  • Compatibility with Sakuli V1.x

How it works

 Sakuli combines Seleniums power for webautomation (in real browsers) with the power of Nut.js to emulate any other possible user-interaction, like opening rich-clients.
